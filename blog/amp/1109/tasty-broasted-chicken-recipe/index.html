Broasted chicken is easy to make. This fried chicken recipe is so perfect which gives you a similar one to KFC fried chicken and it tastes amazing with garlic sauce.

 

Broasted Chicken Recipe - Chicken Broast Recipe

 

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 10 mins

Total time: 20 mins

 

Recipe type: Main

Cuisine: Indian

Serves: 6 servings

 

Ingredients

Oil for deep frying

 

FOR CHICKEN:

Chicken with skin and bones - 1 whole chicken cut into 12 pieces

Salt to taste

Black Pepper to taste

Lemon Juice or vinegar - 1 tablespoon

FOR FLOUR COATING:

All-Purpose Flour / Maida - 2 cups

Cornflour - ¾ cup

Garlic powder - 2 tablespoons

Dried Parsley - 1 tablespoon

Dried Oregano - 2 tablespoons

Dried Basil - 1 tablespoon

Salt to taste

Black Pepper - 2 teaspoon

Chilli Powder - 1 tablespoon

FOR EGG:

Egg - 2

Water - ¼ cup

Salt to taste

 

Instructions

Mix chicken with salt, pepper, and vinegar and set aside.

Take flour in a bowl along with all ingredients given together. Set aside.

Take an egg, salt, water in a bowl and whisk well.

Now take chicken, coat in flour, then dip in egg and then coat in flour. Pack it well.

Set aside in a plate to dry for 5 mins.

Heat oil in a pressure cooker, drop the chicken in and fry for 30 seconds to 45 seconds.

Cover the cooker and pressure cook for 1 or 2 whistles.

Now switch off the flame and let it sit for 5 mins.

Now release the pressure carefully by lifting the whistle with a spoon.

Now open the cooker, and heat it again and fry till the outer layer gets crispy again.

Now drain it and serve hot.
