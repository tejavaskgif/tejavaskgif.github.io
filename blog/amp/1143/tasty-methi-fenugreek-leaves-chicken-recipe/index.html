A super fragrant and tasty chicken dish made using methi leaves. This chicken dish tastes great with rice, roti, phulka as well.

When I was looking for dinner inspiration online, I came across a picture of a spicy chicken curry served with some naan. I got so inspired by that so I made it almost immediately.

This chicken dish is so easy to make, you don't need coconut or any other kinds of stuff. If you use larger chicken pieces then it will be tastier. I used small cut chicken because of what I had on hand.

 

Methi Chicken Recipe - Fenugreek Leaves Chicken Curry Recipe

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 30 mins

Total time: 40 mins

 

Recipe type: Main

Cuisine: Indian

Serves: 4 servings

 

Ingredients

Oil - 4 tablespoons

Shahi Jeera - 1 teaspoon

Onion - 2 large chopped finely

Ginger Garlic Paste - 1 tablespoon

Green Chilli - 2 slit

Tomato - 3 pureed

Chicken - 500 grams

Chilli powder - 2 teaspoons

Kashmiri Chilli Powder - 1 tablespoon

Turmeric Powder - 1 teaspoon

Cumin Powder - 1 teaspoon

Coriander Powder - 2 teaspoon

Garam Masala Powder - 1 teaspoon

Kasuri Methi Leaves - ½ cup

Yogurt - ½ cup

Coriander leaves a handful chopped finely

Salt to taste

 

Instructions

Heat oil in a pan, add shahi jeera.

Add onions and cook till golden brown.

Add in ginger garlic paste and chilies. Saute for a min.

Add in the tomato puree and cook till oil separates.

Add in chicken and stir well

Add in salt and spice powders and mix well.

Add water and cook chicken for 15 to 20 mins.

Now add in whisked yogurt and mix well.

Simmer for 10 mins.

Add in methi leaves and mix well.

Add in garam masala powder and coriander leaves and mix well.

Serve it.
