Lasagna is quite easy to make but it can feed a crowd. All you need is to make some tomato sauce and white sauce and you are good to go. You can make a huge batch of it and make multiple pans of lasagna and freeze it. You can directly bake frozen lasagna and enjoy on busy weeknights.

Chicken Lasagna Recipe

Prep time: 15 mins

Cook time: 1 hour

Total time: 1 hour 15 mins

 

Chicken Lasagna Recipe which is cooked with spicy chicken sauce and creamy white sauce. The cheese on top tastes so delicious.

 

Cuisine: Italian

Serves: 6 servings

 

Ingredients

No-Cook Lasagna Sheets as needed

Mozzarella Cheese as needed

 

FOR WHITE SAUCE:

Butter - ¼ cup

All-Purpose Flour / Maida - ¼ cup

Milk - 2 to 3 cups

Black Pepper Powder - 1 tsp

Salt to taste

 

FOR TOMATO SAUCE:

Olive Oil - 2 tablespoon

Garlic - 2 tablespoon chopped

Onion - 1 chopped finely

Carrot - 1 chopped finely

Tomatoes - 4 to 5 chopped finely

Cooked Chicken - 4 cups chopped finely

Oregano - 2 tsp

Basil - 2 tsp

Salt to taste

Red Chilli Flakes - 2 tsp

Tomato Ketchup - ¼ cup

Coriander leaves for garnishing

 

Instructions

Heat butter in a pan, add flour and stir fry for a min. Add milk slowly and keep mixing till it thickens.

Add in salt, pepper and mix well.

Heat olive oil in a pan, add onions, garlic, carrots, tomatoes and saute for 2 mins.

Cover and cook till tomatoes are cooked.

Add chicken and other spices and mix well.

Add in ketchup and water and mix well. Cook till sauce thickens.

Take a baking dish, spread tomato sauce at the bottom. Place lasagna sheets. Top with white sauce. Top with more tomato sauce, then mozzarella cheese. repeat this two times.

The top layer should be a white sauce. Top with cheese.

Cover with foil and cook for 40 mins in a 180-degree in the oven.

Now remove the cover, place it in grill mode and cook for 5 mins till top is golden brown.

Remove and cool for 5 mins.

Serve.
