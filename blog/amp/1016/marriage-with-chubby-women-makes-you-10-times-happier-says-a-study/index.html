Marriage with chubby women makes you 10 times happier, says a study

By AskGif | Nov 28, 2019

Source : AskGif

  • life-hacks
    •
  • marriage
    •

Everyone, brace yourself! The revelations of recent research have created a buzz in the world of love and relationships. It seems marrying a curvy woman is actually beneficial for your happiness levels and in turn, could be one of the factors behind a happy marriage. Guess what, even science backs up this notion. Read on…

 

​The study

The researchers at the National Autonomous University of Mexico surveyed hundreds of couples and came up with an interesting correlation between a woman’s size and her partner’s happiness level. The study concluded that men who marry chubby women are TEN times happier than all the other men.

 

​Not just this

The study also indicated that men who marry chubby women smile more often than the ones who are in a relationship with thin partners. Plus, the research indicated that plump women are better at dealing with issues in a relationship. Seriously?

 

What about the thin ones?

The researchers delved into the character traits of women and opined thin woman is more on reserved and unfriendly side. In fact, the study referred to them as less expressive which may impact the happiness levels in a relationship.

 

However!

Irrespective of the findings of the study, what matters the most is that all of us own up to our bodies, whatever may be the size. The only thing that makes that matters is that you are fit, healthy and in charge of your own life. You will be able to make your partner happy only when you are at peace with yourself and joyful inside, right?
