Super simple but delicious chicken curry made Palakkad style using shallots, onions and fresh masala. This deep coloured chicken curry not only tastes amazing but is easy to make as well.

This is a simple chicken curry made using fresh spices and shallots. The masala is darker in colour which gives the curry a deep rich colour.

 

 

Palakkad Chicken Curry Recipe

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 45 mins

Total time: 55 mins

 

Recipe type: Main

Cuisine: Indian

Serves: 4 servings

 

Ingredients

Chicken - 1 kg bone-in and cut into small pieces

Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp

Salt to taste

 

FOR ROASTING & GRINDING:

Shallots / Sambar Onion - 15

Dry Red Chillies - 5 to 6

Coriander powder - 3 tablespoons

Red Chilli Powder - 1 tablespoon

Cumin powder - 2 teaspoon

Garam Masala Powder - 1 tablespoon

Turmeric Powder - 1 teaspoon

Coconut Oil - 1 tablespoon+ 3 tablespoon

Onion - 2 large sliced thinly

Curry Leaves a handful

Ginger Garlic paste - 2 tablespoon

Lemon Juice - to taste

Coriander Leaves a handful chopped finely

Curry leaves a sprig

Salt to taste

 

Instructions

Take chicken in a bowl add salt and turmeric and mix well. leave to marinate for 30 mins.

Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a Kadai. Add in shallots and dry red chillies and fry for 3 mins. Remove to a blender.

Now in the same Kadai. Add in spice powders and toast them for 2 to 3 mins.

Remove to the same blender and puree till smooth.

Heat more oil in the Kadai. Add in onions and curry leaves and cook till golden.

Add in ginger garlic paste and mix well.

Add in chicken and mix well.

Cover and cook the chicken till it is half done.

Add in the ground masala and mix well. Cover again and cook for 10 to 15 mins on low flame.

Turn off the heat and add in lemon juice, curry leaves and coriander leaves. Mix well.

Serve it.
