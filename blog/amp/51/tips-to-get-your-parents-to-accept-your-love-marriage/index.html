Tips to get your parents to accept your love marriage.

If you are also one of those young Indians grappling with questions like "Why Indian parents are against love marriage?" or "How to convince parents for love marriage?" , need not worry. We have lined up some expert advice on why our parents oppose love marriage and how to convince them see your point of view!

1. Learn from your friends and family

All you need to do now is to figure out what is the situation with respect to the points and see if the balance is in your favour. I don’t think your family is in a state of mind to accept rational arguments. Taking a calculated gamble is the best way forward. The gamble could be to get married to your love interest against your family’s wishes or break off the relationship in light of your family’s opposition. Obviously, you want to get married to the love of your life!

2. Negotiate like a child

You wouldn’t have noticed something that parents face all the time. It is children and their negotiation tactics. They throw tantrums, win over through sympathy, approach the parent that is likely to say "yes" or pretend to fall sick. Most of the times, they end up getting what they want eventually.

3. Build trust with your parents gradually

From your parents perspective, you have broken their trust and ruined their dreams of getting you married with a person that they believe is suitable. But the question of building trust is not just between you and your parents. A strategy that might work in your favour is to introduce your love interest casually as a friend (if your family is not too conservative in these matters). This will give you and your love interest to build rapport and gain trust. So why is trust important when it comes to convincing parents about your love marriage?

4. Highlight the potential of your soulmate

You must have found the most accomplished partner that you could ever imagine. You might be believing that the person you fell in love with so much more accomplished by anyone that your parents might have lined up for an arranged marriage. But just highlighting that fact that you have caught a "big fish" may not work well. In fact, your parents may turn around and tell your lover is way out of your league and that the marriage will never be successful.

5. Build allies and seek advice

If your parents are against your love marriage, then one of the easiest strategies for convincing them is to build a strong coalition that can champion your cause and provide advice. At the least, they provide much needed moral support when things get heated between you and your parents.

6. The best time to break the news about your love

Telling the news about your love interest to your parents is a nerve-wracking experience for some of us. The response you get from your parent about your love is dependent on several factors such as how open they are to accept such ideas, their prevailing mood.
