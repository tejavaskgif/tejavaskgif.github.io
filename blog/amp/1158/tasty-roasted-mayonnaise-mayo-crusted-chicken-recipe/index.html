Super delicious chicken which is baked to perfection. The chicken has a mayonnaise coating and it tastes amazing.

This is an easy dinner or lunch to put together. It takes less than 10 mins to put together. Once you assembled everything it takes around 15 mins to cook. The chicken is so flavourful and tastes so juicy. The chicken outside has a nice crust in them and the inside is so juicy.

 

Roasted Mayo Chicken Recipe

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 15 mins

Total time: 25 mins

 

Recipe type: Side

Cuisine: Indian

Serves: 2 servings

 

Ingredients

Chicken Breast - 2 slits in half

Mayonnaise - 4 tablespoons

Mustard Sauce - 2 teaspoons

Dried Oregano - 1 teaspoon

Dried Parsley - 1 teaspoon

Salt to taste

Chilli Flakes - 2 teaspoons

Cornflour - 3 tablespoons

Lemon Juice - 2 teaspoons

Breadcrumbs

 

Instructions

Mix all the ingredients together except chicken and breadcrumbs.

Add chicken and mix well. Leave to marinate for 30 mins.

Preheat oven to 250 degrees C. Line a baking pan with foil and grease well with oil.

Place chicken on the pan and top with breadcrumbs if you like.

Pop this in the oven and cook for 10 to 12 mins until chicken is cooked.

Now place it on the top rack on the oven and turn on the grill/broil mode. Cook the chicken for 5 to 6 mins till the top is golden.

Remove and serve it.
