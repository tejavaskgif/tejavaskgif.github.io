Healthy and nutritious sooji porridge mix for babies. It is rich in the carb, protein, and fat which makes a great breakfast for babies.

 

Sooji Cereal Mix for Babies

 

Prep time

10 mins

 

Cook time

15 mins

 

Total time

25 mins

 

Recipe type: Baby Food

Cuisine: Indian

Serves: 2 cups of powder

 

Ingredients

Sooji / Semolina / Rava - 1 cup

Roasted Gram Dal / Pori Kadalai - ½ cup

Cashews - ¼ cup

Cardamom Powder - 1 teaspoon

 

FOR PORRIDGE:

Sooji powder - 2 tablespoon

Water - ½ cup or as needed

Sweetener as needed

 

Instructions

Roast sooji and cool it down.

Roast gram dal and cashews till golden and remove to a bowl and cool it down.

Take it in a blender, add cardamom and powder finely.

Store in an airtight container.

While making porridge, add some powder in a saucepan. add water and cook till thickened.

Add sweetener and mix well.

feed your baby.

 

Notes

1)This can be started after 7 months in babies.

2)Store this in the fridge for a month.
