Vegetable Khichdi ( Rice ) For Babies

By AskGif | Feb 09, 2020

Source : AskGif

  • recipe
    •
  • baby-food
    •

Khichdi is such a healthy one to feed your baby since it has dal in it. It gives the protein which your baby needs. And it is yummy too. I have shown you a few variations in making it. A simple dal khichdi and spinach khichdi, this one is a vegetable khichdi. It is packed full of vegetables and spinach, so much more healthy for your baby.

 

Things to remember before making this

1) Cook rice and dal properly till it is almost mashed up, which helps in easy digestion in babies.

2) Always wash rice and dal very well to remove all the starch and wacky stuff from it.

3) Add garlic and asafoetida helps in digestion in babies.

4) This khichdi is so filling and nutritious meal for your babies.

5) You can start feeding this for babies above 6 months.

6) Always feed this meal for lunch, not for dinner.

7) The consistency of this is up to you. You can mash them with a potato masher, or put them in a blender and make into a fine puree. I make it into a fine puree.

8) Adding ghee enhances the flavor and makes it more nutritious for your baby and helps in weight gaining in babies.

9) Make sure the ghee which you use is pure ones. I make my own ghee.

10) Rice won't cause constipation, allergies in babies. 

 

How to sterilize baby feeding bowls:

 

Start by sterilizing the bowls and spoons which you are going to feed your baby. Take the bowl and spoon in a large saucepan, cover it with water and bring them to a boil. Boil it rapidly for 5 mins. Now you can turn the heat off and leave them to stay in the hot water till use. 

 

 

How to make Rice, Dal & Vegetable Khichdi:

 

Ingredients:

 

Rice – 3 tablespoon

Yellow Moong Dal / Pasi Paruppu – 3 tablespoon

Mixed Vegetables – 1/2 cup chopped (Carrots, Beetroots, Cauliflower, Potato)

Spinach – a handful 

Garlic – 3 fat cloves

Asafoetida / Hing / Kaya Podi – a pinch

Salt to taste

Homemade Ghee – 1 tablespoon

Water – 1 1/2 cup

 

Method:

 

Take rice and dal in a bowl and wash it well. Now drain it and add it into a pressure cooker. Add in chopped vegetables, garlic, asafoetida, salt. Add in water and cover the cooker. Pressure cook this for 5 whistles and simmer it for 5 mins. Switch off the flame and let the steam escape all by itself.

Open the cooker and mash the rice with a potato masher or if you want it to be smooth, you can take that in a blender and puree them. Pour this into a bowl. Drizzle in homemade ghee and mix well. Feed your baby warm.
