Sticky chicken meatballs recipe is one of my favorite. This hardly takes a few minutes to cook. The cool thing about this recipe is you can add whatever spices and herbs you wish in the meatballs. Sticky chicken meatballs recipe is easy to customize as per your taste.

The sauce coating the meatballs is sweet and garlicky. You could add some chili flakes in them if you want some kick. But when making for kids, it is best to avoid it so that they can enjoy it too.

 

Here are few tips for getting perfect, tender and juicy chicken meatballs.

 

1) Add bread soaked in some milk for adding moisture.

2) Don't over mix the meatballs.

3) Don't over fry the meatballs.

4) You could add some water into the meatballs mixture to make it juicy.

5) Grind half chicken breast and half chicken thighs for added moisture.

6) Adding onions and peppers will add moistness to the meatballs.

 

I hope you will love this recipe and let me know if you try it.

Sticky Chicken Meatballs Recipe

 

Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 30 mins

Total time: 40 mins

 

Recipe type: Appetizer

Cuisine: Chinese

Serves: 3 servings

 

Ingredients

For Meatballs :

Minced Chicken - 250 grams

Chilli Powder - ½ teaspoon

Pepper Powder - ½ teaspoon

Coriander Leaves - 2 tablespoons finely chopped

Green Chilli - 1 teaspoon finely chopped

Parmesan Cheese - ½ cup grated

Bread - 1 slice

Milk - 4 tablespoons

Salt to taste

Italian Seasoning - 1 teaspoon

 

FOR SAUCE:

Oil - 1 tablespoon 

Garlic - 2 tablespoons chopped finely

Tomato Ketchup - ¼ cup

Vinegar - 2 tablespoons 

Sugar - 2 tablespoons 

Soy Sauce - 2 tsp

Water - ¼ cup

 

Instructions

Mix all ingredients for meatballs. Heat oil in a pan. Add meatballs and fry till golden. Remove to a plate.

In the same Kadai. Add in oil and saute some garlic. 

Add in ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and water.

Bring it to a boil and cook till syrupy.

Now add in meatball and mix well.

Serve it.

 

Notes

1) You can add any spices or herbs in the meatballs. It is your choice.

2) The garlic in the sauce is what makes this so tasty. So add as much garlic as possible.

3) You can make this and store it in the fridge for up to a week.

4) Adding sesame seeds will make this more appealing and adds a nice crunch.
